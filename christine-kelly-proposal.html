Coverage Plan

Your three days, fully covered

Based on our call, here is what I will cover across Thursday, Friday, and Saturday — every moment from arrival through to the reception.

Thursday
Arrival Day
Venue Scout & Arrival Coverage

I arrive at Brush Creek, scouting light, landscape, and the venue in general ahead of the big day. I'll capture the natural moments of arrival.

Environment & Detail Shots

The landscape of the Mountain West is extraordinary. I'll spend time with the land itself. The kind of establishing images that give your gallery a true sense of place.

Friday
Portrait Day & Rehearsal
2-Hour Portrait Session (Afternoon)

This is our golden hour session — just the two of you in the landscape of Brush Creek. The afternoon light in Wyoming will be extraordinary. No pressure, no posing. Just you, Kelly, and the beginning of your marriage being photographed with intention & direction from my part.

Saturday

Getting Ready Coverage

Cowboy Chapel Ceremony

~100 guests. An intimate ceremony. I'll be unobtrusive and present. Capturing every reaction, every tear, and the moment you become husband and wife.

Reception & Celebration

The families coming together around the table — that's what Kelly is most looking forward to. I'll make sure those moments are documented with the same care as the ceremony. 8 hours total coverage on the day.
